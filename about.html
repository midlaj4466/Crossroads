Crossroads

Everyone comes to the crossroads of their life and our mission is to help the students to choose the right path when they are stuck in the crossroads of their career. Crossroads is a YouTube channel where we constantly broadcast useful and informative content related to technology and career building. Crossroads has crossed ten thousand plus subscribers and eight thousand plus watch hours on YouTube. Crossroads frequently come up with exciting initiatives like 100K Coding Challenge — aims to teach computer programming to 1 lakh students across Kerala, RoadMap — a Q&A platform where students can ask their career related doubts and expert will address their questions and concerns and several other initiatives as time demands.
